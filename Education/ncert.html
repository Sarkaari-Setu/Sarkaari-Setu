CBSE Website User Guide

Learn how to efficiently use the official CBSE website for examinations, results, academics, and student services.

🌐 Step 1 — Open the Official Website

Visit the official CBSE website using your browser. The homepage displays the latest notices, examination updates, public announcements, and circulars.

Tip: Always start from the homepage because important announcements appear there first.

📂 Step 2 — Choose the Correct Section

Select the section based on your requirement.

  • Results – Board examination results.
  • Examinations – Date sheets, admit cards, notices.
  • Academics – Syllabus and sample papers.
  • Student Services – Migration certificate, duplicate documents.
  • School Services – Affiliation and school-related information.

📢 Step 3 — Read the Complete Notification

Do not rely only on headlines. Open the circular and carefully read:

  • Eligibility
  • Important dates
  • Required documents
  • Application process
  • Official instructions

📝 Step 4 — Keep Required Details Ready

Many CBSE services require:

  • Roll Number
  • School Number
  • Admit Card ID
  • Registration Number
  • Date of Birth
Keeping these details ready saves time and prevents mistakes.

📄 Step 5 — Download Important Documents

Download important PDFs such as:

  • Result
  • Date Sheet
  • Sample Papers
  • Circulars
  • Guidelines

Save a copy on your device and print it if required.

⚠️ Step 6 — Verify Information

Always verify that the notice is published on the official CBSE website before following any instructions.

Avoid trusting fake result links, unofficial websites, or social media posts without verification.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Bookmark the official website.
  • Check the homepage regularly during board exams.
  • Download important notices immediately.
  • Keep your credentials ready before checking results.
  • If the website is slow on result day, wait a few minutes and try again.
  • Read every instruction carefully before submitting any application.
