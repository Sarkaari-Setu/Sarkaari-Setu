🌐 NEET UG Application Registration Guide

Quick process to set up your profile on the NTA portal and submit your form.

📋 Application Requirements

Gather these documents before opening the registration portal:

  • ID Proof: Government ID card, Passport, or legal identity card.
  • Contact info: Active Mobile Number and Email ID.
  • Passport Photo: 10KB to 200KB, white background, 80% face visible.
  • Postcard Photo: 4"x6" size, 10KB to 200KB, white background.
  • Sign & Prints: Scanned signature (4KB-30KB) and both hand finger/thumb impressions (10KB-200KB).
  • Certificates: Category (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) or PwBD certificate if applicable (PDF, 50KB-300KB).

Step 1 — New Registration

Click the portal link below and click "NEET UG New Registration".

Go to NTA NEET Application Portal →

Step 2 — Identity Verification

Select your ID type (Aadhaar alternative validation, DigiLocker, or Passport) to get your unique Application Number.

Step 3 — Profile Setup

Log in using your new Application Number and Password. Fill in your personal details and marks.

Step 4 — Select Medium & Centers

Choose your Exam Medium (Language) and pick your 4 preferred Exam Cities.

Step 5 — Upload Documents

Upload your photos, signature, and documents within the required file sizes.

Step 6 — Fee Payment

Pay the application fee online using UPI, Card, or Net Banking, then download your Confirmation Page.

📇 NEET Admit Card Download Guide

How to get your mandatory exam hall ticket and proforma sheet.

📋 Admit Card Requirements

Ensure you have your login credentials ready:

  • NEET UG Application Number.
  • Candidate's Date of Birth (DD/MM/YYYY).

Step 1 — Access the Portal

Click the login link below to go to the NTA download page.

Go to NEET Admit Card Portal →

Step 2 — Secure Login

Type in your Application Number, Date of Birth, and the Security Pin shown on screen.

Step 3 — Execute Download

Click the "Download Admit Card" button.

Step 4 — Verify the Proforma

Save the entire PDF. Make sure it includes the mandatory Postcard Photo Proforma page.

Step 5 — Check Schedule

Check your allocated Exam Center address, Reporting Time, and Gate Closing Time.

Step 6 — Physical Printout

Print all pages in color on clean, standard A4 sheets.

Step 7 — Photo Attachment

Paste your postcard-size photo onto the proforma page before you go to the exam center.

⚖️ OMR Response & Answer Key Challenge Guide

Step-by-step process to object to a wrong key or a misread OMR bubble.

📋 Challenge Requirements & Fees

Review the standard NTA fee structure before submitting an objection:

  • Application ID and Password or Date of Birth.
  • The specific Question ID or OMR question number you want to challenge.
  • An online fee of ₹200 for every single question challenged (non-refundable).

Step 1 — Open Portal Window

Open the link below while the 2-to-3-day NTA challenge window is officially live.

Go to NTA Challenge Portal →

Step 2 — Option Selection

Log in and click on "Apply for Answer Key Challenge" or "OMR Challenge".

Step 3 — Target Question

Pick the question number you want to challenge from the dropdown list.

Step 4 — NCERT Verification

Select the option choice you believe is correct based on official NCERT books.

Step 5 — Compile Proof

Upload your textbook proof images compiled neatly into a single PDF file.

Step 6 — Submission Payment

Click "Save your Claim" and pay the processing fee online to submit.

🏆 NEET Result & All India Rank Scorecard Guide

How to check your final rank, percentile, and save your scorecard.

📋 Results Verification Requirements

Keep your testing credentials ready:

  • NEET Roll Number (printed on your Admit Card).
  • Date of Birth.

Step 1 — Security Validation

Click the results portal link at the bottom of this section.

Go to NEET Results Portal →

Step 2 — Credential Processing

Type in your Roll Number, Date of Birth, and the verification Captcha text.

Step 3 — Access Rank Letter

Click "Submit" to open your official NEET UG Rank Letter / Scorecard.

Step 4 — Score Evaluation

Verify your marks out of 720, overall percentile score, and All India Rank (AIR).

Step 5 — Cutoff Metrics

Check the category cutoff list to ensure you have successfully qualified.

Step 6 — Counseling Documentation

Download and print multiple physical copies. You will need them for both MCC All India counseling and your local State Quota counseling rounds.
