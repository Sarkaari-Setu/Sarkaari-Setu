1. NEET UG Application Registration Guide

Complete instructions to register your profile on the NTA portal and submit your application form properly.

[ REQUIREMENTS ] - Aadhaar Number, Passport, or government identity document for login verification. - Valid Mobile Number & Email ID (Must belong to candidate or parents for critical NTA alerts). - Passport size photograph (10KB to 200KB, white background, 80% face coverage, ears visible). - Postcard size photograph (4"x6", 10KB to 200KB, white background, 80% face coverage). - Scanned Signature (4KB to 30KB) and Left & Right hand fingers/thumb impressions (10KB to 200KB). - Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) and PwBD certificate if applicable (PDF, 50KB to 300KB). [ STEPS TO COMPLETE ] 1. Click the portal link below and select "NEET UG New Registration". 2. Choose your identification type (Aadhaar/DigiLocker/Passport) to generate your Application Number. 3. Log in using your Application Number and chosen password. Fill out personal and qualification details. 4. Choose your preferred Exam Medium (Language) and select your 4 choices of Exam City Centers. 5. Upload all required scanned documents strictly within the NTA's specified dimensions and file sizes. 6. Pay the registration fee online via Net Banking, UPI, or Credit/Debit Card, and download the Confirmation Page.

2. NEET Admit Card Download Guide

How to access and download your mandatory hall ticket, including the proforma attachment.

[ REQUIREMENTS ] - NEET UG Application Number - Candidate's Date of Birth (DD/MM/YYYY) [ STEPS TO COMPLETE ] 1. Navigate to the official login portal using the link provided below. 2. Enter your unique Application Number, Date of Birth, and the displayed Security Pin. 3. Once logged in, click on the "Download Admit Card" button. 4. Note that your admit card contains multiple pages, including the mandatory **Postcard Photo Proforma**. 5. Check your allocated Exam Center, Reporting Time, and Gate Closing Time. 6. Print the entire Admit Card in color on standard A4 sheets. 7. Paste your postcard-size photo onto the proforma page before arriving at the test center.

3. Official NEET Provisional Answer Key

Review the primary paper sets and download the official key directly from our server right here.

[ REQUIREMENTS ] - Your Question Paper Code (e.g., Q1, R1, S1, T1, etc.) - Your personal OMR Response Sheet copy [ STEPS TO COMPLETE ] 1. Click the direct download button below to save the official NEET Answer Key compilation. 2. Open the document and locate the grid specific to your exact test booklet code. 3. Compare the official correct options against your recorded responses. 4. Apply the official NEET Marking Scheme: - Add **+4 marks** for every correct answer. - Deduct **-1 mark** for every incorrect answer (negative marking). - Do not add or deduct points for unanswered questions. 5. Calculate your estimated final score out of the total 720 marks.

4. OMR Response & Answer Key Challenge Guide

The exact process to dispute an answer or a misread OMR bubble on the NTA website.

[ REQUIREMENTS ] - Application ID and Password / Date of Birth. - Specific Question ID or OMR bubble position you want to challenge. - A non-refundable processing fee of ₹200 per question challenged. [ STEPS TO COMPLETE ] 1. Visit the NTA portal link below during the active 2-3 day challenge window. 2. Log into your profile and click on "Apply for Answer Key Challenge" or "OMR Challenge". 3. Select the specific Question Number from the menu corresponding to your booklet code. 4. Check the box of the option you claim is correct according to NCERT textbooks. 5. Upload supporting data or textbook references as a single PDF if challenging the key. 6. Click "Save your Claim" and proceed to pay the processing fee (₹200 per challenge) online.


Go to NTA Challenge Portal →

5. NEET Result & All India Rank Scorecard Guide

How to view your final percentile, All India Rank (AIR), and download your official scorecard.

[ REQUIREMENTS ] - NEET Roll Number (found on your Admit Card) - Date of Birth [ STEPS TO COMPLETE ] 1. Click the official results portal link listed at the bottom of this section. 2. Input your Roll Number, Date of Birth, and the system verification captcha code. 3. Click "Submit" to view your official NEET UG Rank Letter / Scorecard. 4. Verify your total marks (out of 720), overall percentile, and your All India Rank (AIR). 5. Check if you have qualified under the minimum cut-off percentile for your category. 6. Download and print multiple copies of this scorecard. It is mandatory for both 15% All India Quota (MCC) counseling and 85% State Quota medical counseling rounds.
