1. NEET UG Application Registration Guide

Quick steps to set up your profile on the NTA portal and submit your form.

[ REQUIREMENTS ]

  • ID Proof: Aadhaar Card, Passport, or any government ID.
  • Contact info: Active Mobile Number and Email ID.
  • Passport Photo: 10KB to 200KB, white background, 80% face visible.
  • Postcard Photo: 4"x6" size, 10KB to 200KB, white background.
  • Sign & Prints: Scanned signature (4KB-30KB) and both hand finger/thumb impressions (10KB-200KB).
  • Certificates: Category (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) or PwBD certificate if applicable (PDF, 50KB-300KB).

[ STEPS TO COMPLETE ]

  1. Click the portal link below and click "NEET UG New Registration".
  2. Select your ID type (Aadhaar, DigiLocker, or Passport) to get your unique Application Number.
  3. Log in using your new Application Number and Password. Fill in your personal details and marks.
  4. Choose your Exam Medium (Language) and pick your 4 preferred Exam Cities.
  5. Upload your photos, signature, and documents within the required file sizes.
  6. Pay the application fee online using UPI, Card, or Net Banking, then download your Confirmation Page.

2. NEET Admit Card Download Guide

How to get your mandatory exam hall ticket and proforma sheet.

[ REQUIREMENTS ]

  • NEET UG Application Number.
  • Candidate's Date of Birth (DD/MM/YYYY).

[ STEPS TO COMPLETE ]

  1. Click the login link below to go to the NTA download page.
  2. Type in your Application Number, Date of Birth, and the Security Pin shown on screen.
  3. Click the "Download Admit Card" button.
  4. Save the entire PDF. Make sure it includes the Postcard Photo Proforma page.
  5. Check your allocated Exam Center address, Reporting Time, and Gate Closing Time.
  6. Print all pages in color on clean A4 sheets.
  7. Paste your postcard-size photo onto the proforma page before you go to the exam center.
Go to NEET Admit Card Portal →

3. Official NEET Provisional Answer Key

Check your answers and calculate your estimated score right here.

[ REQUIREMENTS ]

  • Your Question Paper Booklet Code (like 50, 60, 70, etc).
  • Your OMR Response Sheet copy.

[ STEPS TO COMPLETE ]

  1. Click the download button below to save the official Answer Key PDF to your device.
  2. Open the file and scroll down to the chart matching your exact booklet code.
  3. Match the official correct options against the bubbles you marked.
  4. Count your score using the official rules:
    • Give yourself +4 marks for every correct answer.
    • Take away -1 mark for every wrong answer.
    • Do not add or subtract points for unattempted questions.
  5. Add up your total score out of 720 marks.

4. OMR Response & Answer Key Challenge Guide

Step-by-step process to object to a wrong key or a misread OMR bubble.

[ REQUIREMENTS ]

  • Application ID and Password or Date of Birth.
  • The specific Question ID or OMR question number you want to challenge.
  • An online fee of ₹200 for every single question challenged (non-refundable).

[ STEPS TO COMPLETE ]

  1. Open the link below while the 2-to-3-day NTA challenge window is open.
  2. Log in and click on "Apply for Answer Key Challenge" or "OMR Challenge".
  3. Pick the question number you want to challenge from the dropdown list.
  4. Select the option choice you believe is correct based on NCERT books.
  5. Upload your textbook proof images compiled into a single PDF file.
  6. Click "Save your Claim" and pay the processing fee online to submit.
Go to NTA Challenge Portal →

5. NEET Result & All India Rank Scorecard Guide

How to check your final rank, percentile, and save your scorecard.

[ REQUIREMENTS ]

  • NEET Roll Number (printed on your Admit Card).
  • Date of Birth.

[ STEPS TO COMPLETE ]

  1. Click the results portal link at the bottom of this section.
  2. Type in your Roll Number, Date of Birth, and the verification Captcha text.
  3. Click "Submit" to open your official NEET UG Rank Letter / Scorecard.
  4. Verify your marks out of 720, overall percentile score, and All India Rank (AIR).
  5. Check the category cutoff list to ensure you have qualified.
  6. Download and print multiple physical copies. You will need them for both MCC All India counseling and your local State Quota counseling rounds.
