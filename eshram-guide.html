eShram Portal User Guide

Learn how to use the official eShram Portal to register as an unorganized worker, update your profile, download your eShram Card, and access government welfare schemes.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official eShram Portal

Open the official eShram Portal. The homepage provides access to worker registration, profile updates, eShram Card download, and information about welfare schemes for unorganized workers.

Tip: Keep your Aadhaar-linked mobile number active, as OTP verification is required for most services.

📝 Step 2 — Register as an Unorganized Worker

If you are eligible, complete the online registration by providing your personal and occupational details.

  • Enter your Aadhaar Number.
  • Verify your mobile number using OTP.
  • Provide personal information.
  • Enter occupation and skill details.
  • Review your application before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Complete Your Profile

Fill in accurate information to ensure your registration is complete.

  • Residential Address
  • Occupation Details
  • Educational Qualification (if applicable)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Nominee Information
Ensure your Aadhaar and bank details are accurate to avoid issues while accessing eligible benefits.

🪪 Step 4 — Download Your eShram Card

After successful registration, download your eShram Card for future reference.

  • Log in to your account.
  • Download the eShram Card (UAN Card).
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print a copy if required.

✏️ Step 5 — Update Your Information

If your details change, update them through the portal.

  • Update Address.
  • Change Occupation.
  • Update Bank Details.
  • Modify Personal Information.
  • Update Contact Information.

🎯 Step 6 — Explore Welfare Schemes

Registered workers can explore information about various government welfare initiatives available for eligible unorganized workers.

  • Social Security Schemes
  • Insurance Benefits
  • Skill Development Programs
  • Employment Opportunities
  • Government Welfare Initiatives
Regularly check the portal for newly announced schemes and updates.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Register only through the official eShram Portal.
  • Use your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.
  • Keep your bank details updated.
  • Download and safely store your eShram Card.
  • Update your profile whenever your occupation or contact details change.
  • Check the portal regularly for new welfare schemes.
Never pay anyone for eShram registration. Registration on the official eShram Portal is free of cost. Do not share your OTP or personal information with unauthorized persons.
