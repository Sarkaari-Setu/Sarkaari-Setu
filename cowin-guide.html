CoWIN Portal User Guide

Learn how to use the official CoWIN Portal to register for vaccination services, download vaccination certificates, manage beneficiaries, and access immunization records.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official CoWIN Portal

Open the official CoWIN Portal. The homepage provides access to vaccination certificates, beneficiary management, appointment booking (when applicable), and other vaccination-related services.

Tip: Keep the mobile number used during vaccination registration ready, as OTP verification is required.

📱 Step 2 — Sign In to Your Account

Log in using your registered mobile number to access your vaccination records.

  • Enter your registered mobile number.
  • Verify your identity using the OTP.
  • Access your beneficiary dashboard.

📅 Step 3 — Book or Manage Vaccination Appointments

If vaccination appointments are available for a particular program, you can manage them through the portal.

  • Search nearby vaccination centres.
  • Select an available date and time.
  • Book or reschedule appointments (where available).
  • View appointment details.
Carry a valid photo ID if required when visiting a vaccination centre.

📄 Step 4 — Download Vaccination Certificate

After successful vaccination, download your official vaccination certificate.

  • View your vaccination history.
  • Download the vaccination certificate.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print the certificate if needed.

👨‍👩‍👧 Step 5 — Manage Beneficiaries

You can manage multiple beneficiaries linked to your registered mobile number.

  • Add eligible beneficiaries.
  • View beneficiary details.
  • Access certificates for each beneficiary.
  • Update beneficiary information (where permitted).

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Keep your registered mobile number active for OTP verification.
  • Download and save your vaccination certificate after each vaccination.
  • Verify all beneficiary details before booking appointments.
  • Use only the official CoWIN Portal for vaccination-related services.
  • Keep your photo ID available when required.
  • Check the portal regularly for updates to vaccination services.
Never share your OTP or personal information with anyone claiming to offer vaccination services. Always use the official CoWIN Portal or authorized government platforms.
