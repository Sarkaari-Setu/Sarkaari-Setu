📋 How to Check Re-NEET 2026 Result

  1. Visit the Official NEET Website

    Open the official NTA NEET portal.

  2. Click on "Re-NEET UG 2026 Result"

    On the homepage, click the Re-NEET UG 2026 Result link once it is available.

  3. Enter Your Login Details
    • Application Number
    • Password or Date of Birth
    • Security Pin (Captcha)
  4. Click "Submit"

    Your Re-NEET scorecard will appear on the screen.

  5. Download Your Scorecard

    Download the PDF and keep a printed copy for counselling and admission.

📄 Verify These Details

  • ✔ Candidate Name
  • ✔ Roll Number
  • ✔ Application Number
  • ✔ Total Marks
  • ✔ Percentile Score
  • ✔ All India Rank (AIR)
  • ✔ Category Rank
  • ✔ Qualifying Status

⚠ Important

If the website is slow due to heavy traffic, wait a few minutes and try again. Always check your result using the official NTA website only.
