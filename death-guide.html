Death Certificate Application Guide

Learn how to apply for a Death Certificate, track your application, download the certificate, and access related citizen services through your State Government or Municipal Corporation portal.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official Portal

Open your State Government's Civil Registration System (CRS), e-District Portal, or Municipal Corporation website. These portals allow citizens to apply for and manage death certificate services online.

Tip: The registration of a death should be completed as early as possible according to the applicable rules in your state or union territory.

📝 Step 2 — Fill the Application Form

Choose the Death Certificate service and carefully complete the online application form.

  • Enter the deceased person's details.
  • Provide the date and place of death.
  • Enter applicant information.
  • Review all information before submission.

📄 Step 3 — Upload Required Documents

Keep scanned copies of the necessary documents ready before applying.

  • Medical Certificate of Cause of Death (if applicable).
  • Hospital Death Report (if available).
  • Identity Proof of the Applicant.
  • Address Proof of the Applicant.
  • Other documents required by your local authority.
Ensure all uploaded documents are clear and legible to avoid delays during verification.

💳 Step 4 — Pay the Application Fee (If Applicable)

Some authorities may charge a fee depending on the application type or delay in registration.

  • Complete the payment using the available online payment methods.
  • Save the payment receipt for future reference.

📋 Step 5 — Track Your Application

After submitting your application, use the application or acknowledgement number to check its status.

  • Track verification progress.
  • Respond to any document requests if needed.
  • Monitor approval status.

📥 Step 6 — Download the Death Certificate

Once approved, download the digitally issued Death Certificate from the portal.

  • Verify all details on the certificate.
  • Save a digital copy.
  • Print copies if required for legal or administrative purposes.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Apply through the official government portal only.
  • Check document requirements before submitting the application.
  • Keep your application number safe.
  • Download and securely store the issued certificate.
  • Verify all personal details before final submission.
  • Contact your local Registrar of Births and Deaths if you need assistance.
Always use the official State Government, Municipal Corporation, or Civil Registration System (CRS) portal for Death Certificate services. Do not share your OTP, payment details, or personal information with unauthorized individuals or websites.
