ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) User Guide

Learn how to use the official ABHA portal to create your ABHA Number, access digital health records, manage your health profile, and securely share medical information with healthcare providers.

🌐 Step 1 — Visit the Official ABHA Portal

Open the official ABHA portal. The homepage provides services to create an ABHA Number, manage your health records, update your profile, and access other digital health services.

Tip: Keep your Aadhaar or Driving Licence and mobile number ready for a quicker registration process.

📝 Step 2 — Create Your ABHA Number

Register for an ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) by verifying your identity.

  • Choose Aadhaar or Driving Licence for registration.
  • Verify your mobile number using OTP.
  • Complete your personal details.
  • Create your ABHA Address (Health ID).

👤 Step 3 — Complete Your Health Profile

After creating your ABHA account, update your health profile for better access to digital health services.

  • Verify personal information.
  • Update contact details.
  • Manage your health profile.
  • Review linked health records.
Ensure your personal details are accurate to avoid issues while accessing healthcare services.

📂 Step 4 — Access & Manage Health Records

ABHA allows you to securely manage your digital health records from participating healthcare providers.

  • View linked medical records.
  • Access prescriptions.
  • Check laboratory reports.
  • Review hospital records.
  • Manage consent for sharing records.

🔗 Step 5 — Share Health Records Securely

Share your health information only when you choose to do so through the consent-based system.

  • Grant consent to healthcare providers.
  • Revoke consent whenever required.
  • Monitor access to your health information.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Keep your registered mobile number active.
  • Review your health records periodically.
  • Share medical records only with trusted healthcare providers.
  • Keep your profile updated if your contact details change.
  • Use only official ABHA and Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) platforms.
  • Understand the consent request before approving access to your records.
Never share your OTP or login credentials with anyone. Your health records are private—only approve consent requests from trusted healthcare providers.
